Latex-modified Concrete (LMC) involves the addition of latex polymers to the concrete mixture, which enhances its flexibility and crack resistance. Polymer-impregnated Concrete (PIC) is created when a polymer substance is injected into the concrete, improving its resistance to chemicals and increasing its lifespan.
